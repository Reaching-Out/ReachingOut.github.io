Love Languages

A group based on the book The Five Love Languages. The group will discuss the five general ways that romantic partners express and

experience love. While many partners express affection to their signifcant other often at times it can fall flat.Ensuring you

know your partners love language enables you to communicate and support each other better and keep love from getting lost in translation.
