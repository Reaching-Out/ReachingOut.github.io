Weekly mental health blurb:

Understanding and accepting your emotions especially those unpleasant ones many of us try to ignore can make a huge

difference in your ability to manage stress and balance your moods. As such learning more about emotions in general

as well as keeping a mood log can help key you into what parts of your life keep you happier and which detract from your life.
